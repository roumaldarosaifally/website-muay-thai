Eight limbs.
One rhythm.

Fists หมัด — Mahd

Jabs, crosses, hooks, uppercuts — borrowed from Western boxing and sharpened for combinations.

Elbows ศอก — Sok

Slashing, spearing, horizontal — the elbow is the razor of Muay Thai. Short-range, bone-on-bone.

Knees เข่า — Kao

Straight knees, curving knees, flying knees. Devastating from the clinch.

Shins แข้ง — Kang

The roundhouse kick landed with the shin, not the foot — conditioned by thousands of pad rounds.
