Do I need my own gear?

We provide pads and heavy bags, but you will need your own hand wraps and gloves for hygiene purposes.

Is it safe for absolute beginners?

Absolutely. Beginners focus strictly on technique on the pads and bags. Sparring is completely optional and closely monitored.
